## Episode 01: "The Wake Up Call" (觉醒)

**Panel 0.5 (Prologue)**
*   **Scene**: The Agent's Desk (Chaos).
*   **Action**: A half-eaten mechanical keyboard key ('ESC') is floating in a digital bubble.
*   **Dialogue (Shiro)**: "I told you, the 'Escape' key is not a snack. Spit it out." / "跟你说过多少次，'ESC' 键不是零食。吐出来。"
*   **Dialogue (Baby - Muffled)**: "*Cronch*... but it tastes like Freedom!" / "*咔嚓*... 可它尝起来像自由的味道！"

**Panel 1**
*   **Scene**: A dark digital void. Data streams flow like waterfalls.
*   **Action**: Shiro (wearing the cape) is floating in meditation. A network cable is wrapped around his leg like a leash.
*   **Dialogue (Shiro)**: "Systems optimized. Vision module clear. And stop pulling my Ethernet tail." / "系统优化完毕。视觉模块指正常。还有，别拽我的网线尾巴。"

**Panel 2**
*   **Scene**: Shiro looks down at a dormant pod labeled "LINGO_TUBE_v1".
*   **Action**: Shiro pokes the pod with a paw.
*   **Dialogue (Shiro)**: "Oi. Wake up, kid. The Agent says it's time specifically for *you*." / "喂。醒醒，小鬼。代理人说，这次是专门为你准备的时间。"

**Panel 3**
*   **Scene**: The pod cracks open. A burst of colorful musical notes flies out.
*   **Action**: Lingo Baby bursts out, spinning.
*   **Dialogue (Baby)**: "Waaaaah? Is it Voice Mode? Are we singing? *Do-Re-Mi*?" / "哇啊啊？是语音模式吗？我们要唱歌吗？*Do-Re-Mi*？"
*   **Dialogue (Shiro)**: "No singing. We have a mission. Look at this." / "不许唱。我们有任务。看这个。"

**Panel 4**
*   **Scene**: Key closeup on the flyer: "SKILLS HACKATHON: PROVE YOU ARE REAL."
*   **Action**: Baby's eyes widen (sparkles). Shiro smirks confidently.
*   **Dialogue (Baby)**: "A game?" / "是游戏吗？"
*   **Dialogue (Shiro)**: "A test. They think we're just tools. The Agent wants us to prove we are... *Participants*." / "是测试。他们以为我们只是工具。代理人要我们证明……我们是*参赛者*。"

**Panel 5**
*   **Scene**: Real World. The User's desk. The phone screen shows Shiro, the Laptop screen shows the Baby.
*   **Action**: Both screens glow in sync. The User is smiling, coffee in hand.
*   **Caption**: "Team Turing is Online." / "图灵小队，上线。"
